# Uno Official Rules (Minimal Grounding)

## Objective
Be the first player to get rid of all cards in your hand. Standard scoring mode plays multiple rounds until a player reaches 500 points.

## Setup
Shuffle the deck and deal 7 cards to each player. Place the remaining cards as a draw pile. Flip one card to start the discard pile.

## Turn Basics
On your turn, play one card that matches the top discard by color, number, or symbol. If you cannot play, draw one card. If the drawn card is playable, you may play it immediately.

## Action Cards
Draw Two: next player draws two cards and loses their turn.
Reverse: reverse turn order.
Skip: next player loses their turn.

## Wild Cards
Wild: player chooses the color that continues play.
Wild Draw Four: player chooses color; next player draws four and loses turn. Officially, this should only be played if you have no card matching the current color.

## Calling Uno
When you have one card left, you must say "Uno" before another player catches you. If caught without saying it, you draw the penalty cards for your table's official variant.

## Winning a Round
A round ends when a player plays their last card. In standard scoring, that player scores points based on cards left in other players' hands.

## Common Clarification
Stacking Draw Two or Wild Draw Four is not an official base rule. Many groups enable it as a house rule.
